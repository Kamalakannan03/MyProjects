About Us

  Sri Venkateswara group of comapanies have started in the year 2000 by Mohan and Ravi. Both have armed with more than 35 years of industry knowledge with specific experience of taking Sri venkateswara group of companies across India. We are driven by a desire to deliver market innovative, technologically advanced, affordable products that provide value for money to the end users . Despite manufacturing and distributing such huge quantities, we maintain consistency in product quality and ensure timely distribution of the same. We also continue to invest and provide Interior desiging for offices, restaurants and houses to our valuable clients.


  With more than 100+ clients, we are specialized in interior solutions and distributor of raw materials such as plywoods, laminates and adhesives to our clients.


  The state-of-the-art factory equipped with skilled man power ensures utmost quality and timely delivery. A process driven delivery mechanism and customer service team ensures hassle free experience in getting all private and public sectors interior needs fulfilled.


  With over a thousands of square feet manufacturing space and a workforce of over 300 employees, we deliver tons of units to market every week. Our key products are "Kings ply, Coasta Ply, Turtle Ply and SV Gold". All products are ISO 9001:14001 certified.
